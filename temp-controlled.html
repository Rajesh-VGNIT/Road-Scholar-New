Temp Controlled

At Road Scholar Transport, we provide the latest technology, equipment and experience to ensure that all storage, shipping, and handling of your temperature-critical shipments meets or exceeds safety and security guidelines.

  • 100% asset based carrier
  • Our strategically placed terminal network, relay capabilities and cross-dock locations allow us to reach 88% of major northeast distribution hubs in under 60 minutes.
  • State-of the-art reefer technology
  • Continuous surveillance
  • Electronic door seals geo-fence and timestamp all door opening and/or closing events
  • All reefers equipped with Orbcomm’s leading-edge trailer tracking and temperature monitoring devices
  • Temperature telematics track and monitor location and temperature of all shipments in real time
  • 24-hour thermal mapping validation by Sensitec
  • 24/7 remote end-to-end temperature monitoring
  • Written and audited temp control oversight protocols for drivers’ immediate response to temperature excursions if they occur.
  • Protocols in place to measure and alert on temperature excursions and to immediately change temperature set points remotely.
  • Complete temperature records of all shipments from pickup to delivery maintained for a period exceeding 12 months
  • Secure access to live tracking of the tractor or trailer transporting your product is provided upon request.
  • Dispatch and maintenance receive an automated early morning report of planned temperature controlled moves.
  • Refrigerated equipment is assigned and pre-cooled when necessary.
  • Additional services requiring access to any point in the U.S., Canada, or Mexico are also available through our brokerage division.
  • Truck, rail, or air freight services are also available.

Count on Road Scholar Transport to be a strategic partner that adheres strictly to your quality management practices.
